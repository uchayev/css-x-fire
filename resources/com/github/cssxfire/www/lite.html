CSS-X-Fire (1.38) for Firebug Lite

Get connected

CSS-X-Fire bookmarklet (drag and drop the link to the bookmarks toolbar).

First load Firebug Lite into the page, then click on the created bookmark for CSS-X-Fire to connect with the IDE.

Tested successfully with Firefox and Google Chrome with Firebug Lite Stable channel. Does not work on IE or Opera due to security restrictions.
